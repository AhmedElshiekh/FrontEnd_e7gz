About E7GZ

E7GZ is a custom built system specifically designed to improve the Egyptian Clinic environment by creating a strict schedule that is closely maintained by a specialized Customer service team every minute throughout the day to ensure quality and efficiency of collecting patients’ information, booking appointments, customizing the clinic’s schedule to suit the physician’s specific needs and present the whole process in the form of a simple app that will be the clinic’s virtual assistant.

How it works

Simply sign up and we will set up all the technical necessities for your clinic for free! After everything is set up your clinic will require patients to call and book appointments in advance and when the patients call your clinic the calls will be forwarded to our customer service call center they will speak with the patients and book their appointments on the system and appointments will automatically appear on your app Login to your E7GZ account through our mobile app and Set your availability in the clinic and the system will strict the appointment booking ability to your predetermined availability. It will automatically show you today’s appointments and give you the ability add or edit existing appointments. You can request to cancel the appointments in any chosen duration of time and the system will automatically notify the customer service call center to call your patients and change their appointments. Fair billing system that will only charge you 5 EGP / appointment to make sure you only pay for the services you get with the smart monthly reports feature. Live chat support available every minute of your clinic working hours to make sure that any possible problems will be solved in a matter of seconds.
